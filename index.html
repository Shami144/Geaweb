About Us

The GAIL Employee Association, proudly affiliated with Bharatiya Mazdoor Sangh, is a trusted organization devoted to representing and safeguarding the interests of GAIL employees. Established with the objective of ensuring fair treatment and promoting employee welfare, the association operates with full recognition under the Code of Discipline and is registered under Regd. No. 4145.

Our central office is situated at A-47, Phase 2, GAIL Complex, Vijaypur, Guna (M.P.) 473112, serving as the hub for our activities and initiatives. We are committed to creating a harmonious work environment, resolving employee grievances, and upholding their rights and benefits. Through collaboration, transparency, and dedication, the GAIL Employee Association continues to be a steadfast partner in the professional journey of every member.
